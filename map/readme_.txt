Inspiration from BloodStrike: ShatterIsland

I wanted to create a battle royal style template for the community, so others can take it and add to it. Creating it in their style would be cool to see. Hope it grows to other cods as well. The final version is different from the original design of what I had envisioned it to be, but nonetheless it still turned out pretty cool, imo. But I'm biased :p

I didn't know what to put on the other side of the map, so I improvised on it by using prefabs.
